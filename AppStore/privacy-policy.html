Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 28, 2026

DreamoraAiPro is designed to help you track and analyze your dreams while improving your sleep quality. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

Dream Journal Entries: When you record dreams, the text you enter is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Sleep Tracking Data: If you enable sleep tracking features, the sleep duration and quality metrics are stored locally on your device.

App Usage Information: We may collect anonymized, aggregated usage data to help improve the app experience. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

In-App Purchases: DreamoraAiPro offers premium subscriptions through Apple App Store In-App Purchases. All payment processing is handled by Apple - we do not collect or store your payment information.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the dream journal and sleep tracking features
  • To deliver AI-powered dream analysis (processing is performed on-device when possible)
  • To send push notifications for sleep reminders (if enabled)
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

Data Storage

All personal data, including dream journal entries and sleep tracking data, is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. We do not have access to this data. You can delete your data at any time by deleting the app.

Third-Party Services

DreamoraAiPro may use third-party services for:

  • Apple Store Connect: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • Firebase (if configured): For crash reporting and analytics

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

DreamoraAiPro is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
